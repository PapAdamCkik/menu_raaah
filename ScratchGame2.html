ScratchGame2 is the team's biggest project and the sequel of ScratchGame. It is a turn-based RGB, where you have to defeat zombies by either killing them or make them weak enough to spare their lives.

The game takes place after the events of ScratchGame. After defeating one of the key people of the organisation behind the apocalypse, the protagonist, SemenDemon enters the facility of the organisation, but after getting inside he loses consciousness and wakes up in a lab. He needs to escape and destroy the facility, but something weird is going on in the background.
